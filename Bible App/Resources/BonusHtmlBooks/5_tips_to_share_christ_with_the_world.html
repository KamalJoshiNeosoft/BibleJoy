5 Tips to Share Christ with the World

 

Matthew 28:19 states, ¨Go ye therefore, and teach all nations….¨This verse is one of the many times that we are reminded of the need to instruct other people about Christ and His teachings, but how do we get to this point? There are some common ways we can share Christ with the world before getting into scripture. Now, you may ask: why wouldn't I share scripture with people whom I meet?  You certainly can, but there are times that people need to see Christ in you more than they need to hear how Christ is in you.

 

  1. Be kind.  Kindness is something that costs nothing, but yields great dividends.  Ephesians 4:32 reminds us to ¨Be kind and compassionate to one another, forgiving each other, just as in Christ, God forgave you.¨  When you are out shopping or at a restaurant and witness an employee having a bad attitude, meeting their anger or hostility with your own will accomplish nothing.  Instead, be kind.  This simple gesture can be a powerful statement that Christ lives within you.

 

  1. Create relationships.  Some people are more resistant to hearing about Christ than others.  One way to tell these people about Christ is to build relationships.  If there is a local drug addiction shelter in your area or a homeless shelter, see if you can donate meals and/or write letters with the residents.  This simple gesture can be a way to build relationships.  Through your commitment to build a genuine relationship with these individuals, you are already allowing them to see Christ's work inside you. 

 

  1. Be set apart.  Romans 12:2 states, ¨And be not conformed to this world, but be ye transformed by the renewing of your mind, that ye may prove what is that good and acceptable, and perfect, will of God.¨  Your actions, your words, and your daily life is a living testimony of the transforming power of Jesus Christ.  Live a life that shows you have been set apart and that is why you make the choices you make which are grounded in God's Word.

 

  1. Pray for opportunity and embrace it when it comes.  Colossians 4:5 reads, ¨Be wise in the way you act toward outsiders; make the most of every opportunity.¨  This verse teaches us that we have limited time to share Christ with the world.  Pray that God will place people in your path to share Him with.  When those people are in your presence, embrace that opportunity.

 

  1. Keep it simple.  The Holy Bible is a complex book that is meant to challenge us to become stronger, holier people, but this is a progressive process that will never be finished until we reach Heaven.  When sharing Christ with the world, keep it simple!  Tell them about God's peace and life.  Share the problem that comes from being separated from God.  Talk about the cross as the ultimate remedy.  Explain the response we have thanks to His ultimate sacrifice - praying to receive Christ.
