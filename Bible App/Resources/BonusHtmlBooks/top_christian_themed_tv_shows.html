Top Christian Themed TV Shows

 

With people spending more time at home than ever before, the family may gather around the TV a bit more frequently.  It is important to follow the instruction in Psalm 101:3:  “I will not set before my eyes anything that is worthless. I hate the work of those who fall away; it shall not cling to me.”  To help your family choose TV shows that are Christian themed, we have provided six options.

 

  1. The Encounter Series.  Viewers will watch characters have encounters with Jesus in their daily lives.  For example, a store clerk may have an encounter with Jesus, who appears in disguise.  He is there to help the character through some current or past problem.  Based on the original movie called “The Encounter”, each episode brings with it the question:  will the person having the encounter make the right choice? 

 

  1. Sons of Thunder.  A biker doing God’s work?  In this new series, viewers meet Simon.  He has made some mistakes in his life, but now tries to help people God places in his path.  Each person he helps is a way for him to try to atone for his past mistakes.

 

  1. The King’s Messengers.  This is a mini-series that places two refugees front and center in a war-torn country of Zanora.  This fictional setting is where David and Anwaar must learn the value of compromise, teamwork, and friendship if they are going to outwit the Resistance Army.  Their journey takes them farther than the show’s events may reveal, since both characters are on a faith journey to deepen their spiritual walk.

 

  1. The Dream Motel. Filled with questions and somewhat confusion, the characters on The Dream Motel have decisions to make.  Help in making those decisions is supplied from past, present, and future events.  Each guest finds that hope makes the impossible possible. 

 

  1. Ties That Bind. Set in Seattle, this Canadian series presents a tragic family situation.  The detective has to make a difficult decision between morals and family.  After her decision, she finds herself with two teenagers that do not easily blend into her Christian home.  These two teens are not wanting to give in to the stability, pray-centered life that her children are accustomed to, making the plot line thicken. Noted to be a fast-paced drama, there is lots of suspense which keeps viewers wondering what will happen next.

 

  1. Pure Talk.  This Christian talk show presents sensitive issues that may be on the heart of Christians throughout today’s culture. A collaborative project by Pureflix.com and Faithwire.com, viewers will see various spotlighted guests who tell their stories of how God became Lord over their lives.  A variety of topics of interest can be found on this new series.
