Top Christian Themed Movies

 

Are you looking for something to watch that has a Christian theme? These movies are from various time periods, but they all carry a strong message related to Christian principles.

 

  1. The Passion of the Christ. This popular Biblical drama was released in 2004 and continues to captivate audiences. The epic drama uses the New Testament Gospels of Matthew, Mark, Luke, and John to depict the Passion of Jesus. In addition to drawing on devoutly religious accounts including the Friday of Sorrows. The final twelve hours of Jesus’ life are the film’s primary focus.

 

  1. Overcomer.  The Kendrick brothers have made a name for themselves and their commitment to releasing powerful movies with equally powerful Christian lessons taught through daily struggles.  In Overcomer, John Harrison, a high school basketball coach, faces a lot of uncertainty.  The town’s main employer closes and hundreds of people leave town, leaving the Coach without enough players for basketball.  With strong reluctance, John agrees to coach cross-country.  His view of the sport is quickly changed when he meets a runner who needs him as much as he needs her.  Watch this heartwarming story with your family to gain insight into how perspective and God’s grace are powerful tools!

 

  1. Fireproof.  This movie was released in 2008 and tells the story of a young married couple who are experiencing marital difficulties.  Viewers will meet Caleb and Catherine who have to learn the art of compromise and ultimately the power of Christ in their relationship.  The Love Dare is a huge part of the book and has since been created into a book that has changed and challenged married couples to deepen their commitment to each other and to Christ. 

 

  1. I Still Believe.  Jeremy Camp, a popular Christian musician, is the real life inspiration for this romantic biopic. He and his first wife faced a heart wrenching tragedy that forever altered their love story.  Viewers will see how God’s love was represented throughout their commitment to each other for better and for worse, even before they were married.

 

  1. One Night with the King.  Released in 2006, this movie takes viewers inside the Old Testament book of Esther.  This young woman is brought to the forefront because of her courageous spirit, obedient nature, and strong love.  Through these admirable traits, she is able to save her entire nature from annihilation.  Viewers will enjoy the way this Biblical story comes to life on the big screen.

 

  1. Heaven is for Real.  Released in 2014, this movie takes viewers inside the life of a young boy who believes he saw Heaven during an emergency surgery.  Following his appendectomy, he tells his family about meeting God, angels, and family members who were in a place where “nobody is old” and “nobody wears glasses.”
  2. War Room.  Another Kendrick brothers creation, this movie is thematically centered around temptation, failing marriage, and life struggles.  Viewers are challenged to create their own war-room following the epic story that unfolds as a result of one person’s challenge of war-room prayer.
